Do I need the physical amiibo to use the app?+

No. You can browse the entire catalogue and tap to mark anything as owned or wishlisted without any hardware. The NFC scan is an optional extra that verifies a figure you physically own by reading its chip.

What does "Dex-Verified" mean?+

When you hold a real amiibo to your iPhone and the app reads its NFC tag, that figure gets a verified seal and a scan date. It's a way to distinguish figures you've physically confirmed from ones you've simply checked off.

Which devices are supported?+

iPhone on iOS 17 or later. NFC scanning needs an iPhone with Core NFC (iPhone 7 and newer). Browsing, shelves and achievements work on any supported iPhone.

Is my collection data private?+

Yes. Your collection lives on your device. Game Center leaderboards are strictly opt-in, and even then only anonymous aggregate counts (like "figures owned") are shared — never which specific amiibo you have. See the privacy summary.

Are the shopping links affiliate links?+

Some "Buy" links may be affiliate links that help support development, at no extra cost to you. This is disclosed in the app next to the buttons and in Settings.

Privacy

Your collection is yours.

The short version. AmiiboDex stores your collection on your device. It does not require an account and does not sell your data. Optional Game Center leaderboards share only anonymous aggregate totals, and only if you turn them on. NFC scanning happens locally on your iPhone. Shopping links may be affiliate links, disclosed in-app.
