AmiiboDex

Privacy Policy

Last updated 13 July 2026

AmiiboDex is an app for cataloguing your amiibo collection. This policy explains what information the app does and doesn't handle. In short: your collection stays on your device, there's no account, and the app doesn't track you.

The short version

No sign-up, no login, no personal profile. Your collection data lives on your device. We don't collect analytics, we don't serve ads, and we don't sell data — because we don't have it.

Information the app collects

AmiiboDex does not require an account and does not ask you for personal information such as your name, email address, or location. The app has no sign-up or login.

Your collection data

The choices you make in the app — which amiibo you mark as owned, needed, or wishlisted, the figures you've scanned, and your progress and achievements — are stored locally on your device. This data is under your control and is removed if you delete the app.

NFC scanning

When you hold a physical amiibo to your iPhone, the app reads the figure's NFC tag solely to identify it and mark it as verified in your collection. This reading happens on your device.

Game Center & leaderboards

Leaderboards are strictly opt-in. If you choose to participate, ranking is handled through Apple's Game Center, and only anonymous, aggregate figures (such as how many amiibo you've catalogued) are used for ranking — never a list of which specific amiibo you own. Game Center is an Apple service governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. If you don't opt in, no ranking data leaves your device.

Shopping & affiliate links

Some amiibo may include "Buy" links to third-party retailers to help you find figures you're missing. Some of these may be affiliate links that support development at no extra cost to you; this is disclosed in the app next to the buttons. Opening a link takes you to an external website that has its own privacy practices, which we don't control.

Analytics, advertising & tracking

AmiiboDex does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Children's privacy

AmiiboDex does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Data security

Because your collection data stays on your device, its security is protected by your device's own safeguards, such as your passcode and device encryption.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@amiibodex.app and a real person will reply.
